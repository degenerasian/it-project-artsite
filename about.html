About the Artist

PROFESSIONAL BACKGROUND:

  • Graphic artist and illustrator - Mabuhay Magazine, inflight publication of Philippine Airlines (1989-1993)
  • Art director - The Saipan Tribune, top-selling daily newspaper in the Northern Mariana Islands (1994-1996)
  • Publisher - Local Color travel magazine
  • Freelance graphic designer
  • Professional photographer

  • Ricco Renzo, LRI Plaza, Makati City
  • Galerie Astra, LRI Plaza, Makati City
  • Art Center, SM Megamall, Pasig City
  • Panagbenga at the Market! Market! Activity Center, Taguig City
  • Laguna Expo, Alabang Town Center & Glorietta Activity Center
AFFILIATIONS:
